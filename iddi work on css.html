Cavendish University Uganda (CUU) is a premier private university dedicated to providing high-quality, student-centered education. Established with a commitment to academic excellence and innovation, CUU equips students with practical skills, knowledge, and a global perspective to thrive in today's competitive world. With a focus on modern learning approaches, industry-driven programs, and a diverse student community, CUU fosters an environment that nurtures leadership, creativity, and critical thinking.

About CUU

Mission & Vision

  1. Mission
    Cavendish University Uganda (CUU) is dedicated to transforming students into responsible, educated, and skilled professionals through innovative, flexible, and accessible education.
  2. Vision:
    To be a model university that delivers quality education and produces graduates who positively impact their communities and the world.
  3. History:
    2008: CUU was established as a private university in Uganda. 2010: The university introduced online and blended learning programs.
